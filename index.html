This is my first article

Birds (Aves) are a group of vertebrates which evolved from dinosaurs. They are endothermic, with feathers. Modern birds are toothless: they have beaked jaws.

Birds live all over the world. They range in size from the 5 cm (2 in) bee hummingbird to the 2.70 m (9 ft) ostrich. They are the class of tetrapods with the most living species: about ten thousand.

Birds are the closest living relatives of the Crocodilia. The fossil record shows that birds evolved from feathered theropod dinosaurs. Modern birds are not descended from Archaeopteryx.
